Paradise is the home of a 2.5 million years stone age caves. We work very closely with the Museums of Kenya in the preservation of the caves. The caves have unique stone-age artifacts known as obsidian rock which was used by the stone-age people for hunting.

Paradise lost has one of the largest man made lake in Kiambu county. The lake has over ten million liters of water. It is equipped with over 25 boats with a carrying capacity of over 150 people .

Paradise lost has over 50 kilometers of natural and tranquil riding trails. We have over fifty mountain bikes for hire. Our bikes are for both adults and children at a minimal price!
