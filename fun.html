Boat rides

Paradise lost has one of the largest man made lake in Kiambu county. The lake has over ten million liters of water. It is equipped with over 25 boats with a carrying capacity of over 150 people . We have resident life guards on duty all through the day, life jackets is a must to wear while enjoying your boat rides

Boat rides cost Kshs. 200 per person

The lake is well maintained and clean.
